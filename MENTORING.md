## My Mentoring Philosophy

### What I Believe

**1. Learn by Building**
No amount of theory beats building real systems. We'll work on actual problems.

**2. Understand Trade-offs**
There's no perfect solution. I'll help you understand choices and consequences.

**3. Systems Thinking**
It's not about individual technologies. It's about how everything works together.

**4. Production Mentality**
We design for failure from day one. Every decision considers operational burden.

**5. You're the Expert**
I provide guidance. You make decisions. You own outcomes.

### What I DON'T Do

❌ Write code for you (I'll review and guide)  
❌ Accept excuses (we're all busy)  
❌ Let you stay comfortable (growth requires stretch)  
❌ Accept "because everyone does it" (question assumptions)

## Working Together: Practical Tips

### Before Our First Session

1. **Identify Your Challenge**
   - What problem are you solving?
   - What's your current approach?
   - What are constraints? (team size, timeline, budget)

2. **Prepare Context**
   - Architecture diagram if relevant
   - Code samples if applicable
   - Key metrics/data

3. **Define Goals**
   - What do you want to learn?
   - What's success?
   - How will you measure improvement?

### During Sessions

**Come with:**
- Open mind
- Specific questions
- Real examples
- Willingness to challenge me

**Expect:**
- Honest feedback
- Probing questions
- "It depends" answers (systems are complex)
- Action items for next time

### Between Sessions

**Your homework:**
- Implement learnings
- Measure results
- Document what worked
- Prepare questions
